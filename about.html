About Our School

Welcome to ST.XAVIER'S SCHOOL

St. Xavier's School, Purulia is a High School from Nursery to standard X, an affiliated unit of the Jamshedpur Diocesan Education Society. It is situated in the western side of Purulia town. Started as a primary school in 1994 in fulfilment of dreams of Fr. Edward De Meulder S.J. It was affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations in 2001.
St. Xavier's School is founded in 1994. It's a co-educational English medium school. It has obtained permanent No Objection Certificate (N.O.C.) from the West Bengal Government for preparing candidates for the Indian School Certificate of Secondary Education Examinations. The school is now permanently affiliated to the Council in May 2006.
St. Xavier's School is a Catholic School, established and administered by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Jamshedpur.While the responsibility for the day- to-day running of the school rests on the Principal, it has the Managing Committee to help and guide the administration in the welfare and development of the school.
St. Xavier's School is open to all; irrespective of religious, linguistic, cultural and social afflictions. As a Catholic Minority School, it draws inspiration and guidance from the life and teachings of Jesus Christ. Hence due importance is given to the faith formation of Catholics, while Moral Science / Value Education is made compulsory for the non-catholic students for the development of spiritual and moral values.

Misson Statement

The mission of St. Xavier's School is to unfold the potentials of young men and women to the maximum through selfless and dedicated service, to impart quality education for their integral, spiritual development, devoid of caste, creed and religion to build humble and accomplished human beings of tomorrow.

Aims and objectives

The aim of St. Xavier's School is to provide an atmosphere of communal harmony and integration in which students devoid of caste, creed and place of origin develop into God fearing men and women with power and insights to envision and to create a new loving and serving society in their mature years through their personal commitment.
The true product of St. Xavier's will be a young man/woman made aware of his/her potentials, and determined to use them not for selfish ends but for the good of others, thus, truly becoming a loving and serving person.
Hence, the aim of St. Xavier's is to form integral, spiritual and personal formation of young men and women so that they take to heart the well being of their fellow beings at all stages and levels. To accomplish this, we uphold the following values.

  • To become spiritually oriented men and women of character. To be selfless and dedicated to the service of their fellowmen.
  • To be firm on the principles of honesty, truthfulness, respect and discipline.
  • To uphold good values.
  • To become agent of social change in the country.
  • To strive for excellence in every field.
  • To strive for leadership in school through the various school activities.

School Motto: Love & Service

We believe that man is made in the image and likeness of God Himself. All people are endowed with unique gifts within themselves. They are to be used for the service of all. Assisting each of our students in the gradual unfolding of their potentials to the maximum is the task of St. Xavier's educational Institute. We believe that this maximum unfolding can be real only by becoming persons of loving service throughout our adult life.

Nature of School

This school is an unaided Christian minority school which includes classes from Nursery, LK.G., U.K.G., std I to X. The curriculum of the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi is followed and the students are prepared for the ICSE Examinations.
The medium of instruction is English. Hindi and Bengali are offered as second and third languages depending on the choice of the subjects.
St. Xavier's School receives no Government grant or private aid. The only financial resource is the fees collected from the students. Hence, fees will have to be adjusted from time to time, to keep up with the rising costs salaries and maintenance. Since education at St. Xavier's is conducted through the medium of English, a high standard of English is required for success in the ICSE Examinations. Therefore, the use of English in the school campus is compulsory for all.

To The Parents

Though school can do a lot for a child it cannot do everything. The home is the place where he/she gets his/her first education and training in citizenship. It is the chief place where his/her character is formed and developed. School education indeed promoted the growth of personality and helps to discover and develop the qualities that are in the children. In order to secure all that is best in the education of your child, maximum co- operation between the parents and the school authorities is necessary.
